Welcome

Welcome to the Soft Robotics Lab Series — a hands‑on guide to designing and simulating soft robotic actuators using ANSYS Mechanical and MATLAB (SoRoSim). Through curated, bite‑sized labs you’ll master geometry setup, material choices, boundary conditions, and performance optimisation.

Project Overview

The series walks you from first principles to advanced concepts. By the end, you’ll have practical experience in:

  • Hyper‑elastic material modelling & meshing strategies in ANSYS
  • Applying loads, pressures and BCs correctly
  • Building multi‑finger or fibre‑reinforced assemblies
  • Post‑processing & design optimisation
  • Leveraging SoRoSim for continuum soft‑robotic simulation

Lab 1 | Pneumatic Gripper

Objective

Model a four‑finger pneumatic gripper capable of lifting a cylindrical object.

Expected Outcome

Gain fluency with ANSYS Workbench: geometry, meshing, material assignment and boundary conditions.

Lab 4 | SoRoSim (MATLAB)

SoRoSim models soft links as Cosserat rods using the Geometric Variable Strain method.

Quick Quiz

What is the primary purpose of SoRoSim?
