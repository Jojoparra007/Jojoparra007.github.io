Welcome

Welcome to the Soft Robotics Lab Series — a hands-on guide to designing and simulating soft robotic actuators using ANSYS Mechanical and MATLAB (SoRoSim).

Project Overview

By the end of this series you will know how to:

  • Build hyper-elastic material models and robust FE meshes.
  • Apply loads, pressures and realistic boundary conditions.
  • Create multi-finger and fibre-reinforced actuators.
  • Post-process performance metrics for optimisation.
  • Leverage SoRoSim for continuum soft-robot simulations.

Lab 1 | Pneumatic Gripper

Objective

Model a four-finger pneumatic gripper capable of lifting a cylindrical object.

Expected Outcome

Fluency with ANSYS Workbench geometry, meshing, material assignment and BCs.
