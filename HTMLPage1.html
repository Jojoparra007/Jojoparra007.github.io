About Me

I'm Jose Parra, an aerospace engineer with a strong foundation in both theoretical knowledge and hands-on experience. I graduated with a degree in Aerospace Engineering, where I developed a deep expertise in computational fluid dynamics, wind energy analysis, and systems modeling.
